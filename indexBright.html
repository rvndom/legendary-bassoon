FAQ



How do i send my song for mixing?


If your're using Pro Tools, Logic or Studio One you can send the session file.(please include all audio files) The only thing you must do is convert any midi or virtual instrument tracks to audio. I dont always have the same synths oder sound banks.


How do i send my song for mastering?


When you sending your two-track mix for mastering, please send as 24 or 16 bit .WAV file. Turn off all compression, EQ and limiting on the master fader. MP3 files are not acceptable for high quality mastering, and converting them in itunes will not change this. ou must bounce or export diectly from your mixing software to archieve a true 24bit .WAV file. When your ready, make payment and you will receive a link to upload.


How long will it take to get my song back?


For single song mixes, and mastering projects i return songs in 3-4 business days. For full albums it will take about a week or week and a half depending on how intensive the tracks are. if you are in rush, please let me know and we can find a way to expedite the process for a small fee.
